T-REX Entertainment Productions is an independent Filipino company committed to making productions that matter.

We aspire to make films, music, and various entertainment media with original content that are both commercially viable and of excellent quality; content that are created mostly by Filipino talents. Our produced media seeks to treat its audience with respect by shaping consciousness, honing talents, and by creating impactful body of work.

We are looking forward for the responsibility in producing award-winning and top-grosser films, and in creating a collection of relevant entertainment media. We will strive to build reputation that centers on the passion and ingenuity of our creative industry.

Our History

T-Rex Entertainment Productions began with restauranteur and businessman Rex Tiri in 2015. Rex Tiri dared to dive into the unfamiliar waters of film in the first movie he produced, Esoterika: Maynila by Elwood Perez.

What first started as a risk became a growing dream for Rex Tiri. He decided to continue producing films that show and support the artistry of filipino film making, through inspiring stories.

Inspired by the words and masterful storytelling of the late director Gil Portes, Rex Tiri produced Ang Hapis at Himagsik ni Hermano Puli and Moonlight Over Baler. Since 2016, Rex Tiri formed a team that would eventually become T-Rex Entertainment Productions.

Starting 2017, more films from T-Rex Entertainment are being produced and promoted by its honed creative team. Following the success of Patay Na Si Hesus in the box office, T-Rex Entertainment continues to stay by its creed of producing films of sincerity and substance, while being commercially profitable.
